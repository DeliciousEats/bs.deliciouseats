    Our Story

    Founded in 2023, Delicious Eats began with a simple passion and vision of cooking. With the goal to serve quality dishes made with efforts and love, it started all with a simple observations. Elias and his friends Martin and Jomel was no stranger to hard work. Selling homemade cuisines just to get by. To provide for their own expenses and making their life much easier.

    Growing up, Manila was full of food stalls, street vendors, and small food businesses that are affordable but usually don't last long. As an IT student, Elias and his friends figured out they could incorporate technology to promote their own product further expanding their "market" and introducing their crafts efficiently online.

    What started as a simple business just to get by, has grown into a beloved establishment known for its wonderful recipes. Whether you're dining in, ordering takeout, or having us cater your special event, you can be assured that we put the same efforts, care, and attention into every dish we serve!
